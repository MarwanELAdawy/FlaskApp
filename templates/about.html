Fake News Detector is created and managed by a team of students in the Faculty of Computer
and Information science at Ain Shams University.

We are helping people to avoid fake news that is published on social media specially on facebook
usually with the intent to mislead in order to damage a community or person, create chaos, gain
financially or politically and instigate religious or ethnic groups against innocent victims.
